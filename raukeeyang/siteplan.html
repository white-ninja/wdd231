Raukeeyang Integrated Farm Site Plan

This site plan is a polished, globally-minded outline for the existing Raukeeyang website. It documents the brand goals, page architecture, design system, and deployment checklist for the farm’s online presence.

Project Vision

The Raukeeyang website will position the farm as a trusted source of livestock, vegetables, eggs, and agricultural feed. Its purpose is to make product discovery easy, communicate quality, and convert visitors into enquiries through a simple contact journey.

Primary Objectives

  • Showcase farm products and livestock clearly with persuasive content and strong visuals.
  • Increase customer confidence through a consistent brand voice and farm value messaging.
  • Simplify the enquiry process with clear calls-to-action and a user-friendly contact page.
  • Prepare the site for deployment with responsive design, optimized assets, and SEO best practices.

Target Users

  1. Local consumers searching for fresh, farm-produced food for home cooking.
  2. Retailers and restaurateurs requiring reliable livestock and produce supply.
  3. Bulk buyers and event planners seeking dependable farm products for sale or delivery.

Site Map

  • Home — hero section, farm values, product preview, trust elements, and CTA.
  • Products — livestock and produce catalogue with enquiry links to contact.html.
  • Contact — contact form, address, phone, email, and business hours.
  • Thank You — confirmation page after successful form submission.

This plan maps directly to the existing site pages and assets in css/, js/, images/, and data/.

Content Strategy

  • Use product-focused copy that highlights quality, sustainability, and farm reliability.
  • Keep messaging concise and consistent across all pages.
  • Emphasize strong, action-oriented CTAs such as Explore Products and Enquire Now.
  • Use clear product categories and benefit-led descriptions for livestock and farm produce.

Design System

The design follows the current pack of brand colors and typography used in the Raukeeyang project. This ensures visual coherence and a professional farm brand impression.

#2e7d32 — primary brand green

#1b5e20 — accent and hover

#f5f8f5 — light background

#ffffff — clean content surface

Accessibility

  • Use semantic HTML, heading structure, and descriptive image alt text.
  • Maintain strong contrast and legible text across page backgrounds.
  • Ensure navigation and forms are touch-friendly and accessible by keyboard.
  • Keep the contact form simple with clear labels and input focus states.

SEO & Performance

  • Include metadata for page titles, descriptions, Open Graph, and theme color.
  • Optimize images as WebP and preload the hero image for faster loading.
  • Use clean internal links between home, products, and contact pages.
  • Deploy on GitHub Pages with indexing enabled for search visibility.

Deployment Checklist

  • Verify product details, contact information, and page copy.
  • Test layout responsiveness on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
  • Confirm all navigation links and enquiry form actions are functional.
  • Push changes to GitHub and enable GitHub Pages from the repository root.

This plan is aligned with the current Raukeeyang project, its pages, and brand presentation. Use it as the deployment-ready reference for the farm website.
